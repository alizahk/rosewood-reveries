About Us

As a small business committed to sustainability, we’re not just about creating perfect bouquets— we follow an eco-friendly approach in everything we do. We prioritize sustainability throughout our shop, from sourcing to packaging. Our locally sourced blooms come from trusted local farms that are extremely dedicated and have been providing for us for many years, whom we deeply appreciate. Additionally, our packaging is not only recyclable but also biodegradable, an eco-friendly touch that sets us apart from many others in our field of business. We also ensure that no harmful pesticides are used, embracing ethical and environmentally practices.

Another thing we’re proud of is our same-day and next-day delivery options—perfect for last-minute gifts. You can place an order in-store, online, or reach out through the contact details listed on our website. To take advantage of this service, simply subscribe to our floral delivery plan, ensuring your flowers arrive quickly and directly to your door. As a first-time subscriber, you’ll receive an exclusive discount as our way of saying thank you! For more details, feel free to contact us or speak with an in-store associate.

Bouquet Care Guide

Upon purchasing our bouquets, there are a few important tips we'd like to share with our customers to help extend the life of your flowers. Be sure to trim the stems at an angle every few days. Also, change the water regularly to prevent bacteria buildup. Lastly, keep your flowers away from direct sunlight and heat. With every purchase, you’ll receive a care guide free of charge, featuring these tips along with visual diagrams to make it easy to remember.
